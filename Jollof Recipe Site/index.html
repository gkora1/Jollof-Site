Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof is a Ghanaian dish, but many other countries have their own style of making jollof

Total Time to cook: 45 minutes to an hour thirty

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 cup of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of onions
  • 1/2 cup of bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup of tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1/2 cup of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of ginger
  • 1/2 cup of thyme
  • 1/2 cup of curry powder
  • 1/2 cup of salt
  • 1/2 cup of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and set aside
  2. Blend the onions, bell peppers, tomatoes, scotch bonnet peppers, garlic, and ginger together
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pot

    Heat the oil for 5 minutes

  4. Add the blended mixture to the pot and cook for 10 minutes

    Stir occasionally for 10 minutes

  5. Add the tomato paste, chicken broth, water, thyme, curry powder, salt, and black pepper to the pot
  6. Cook for 10 minutes
  7. Add the rice to the pot and stir
  8. Cover the pot and cook for 20 minutes
  9. Stir the rice and cook for an additional 10 minutes
  10. Enjoy your jollof rice!
